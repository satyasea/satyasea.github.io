• Beaufort

    • The Beaufort sea weather app translates hard to understand raw data from multiple buoys into meaningful sea condition observations and warnings.

    App was previously published on Android Market by Androids of Mars as Mars Sea Conditions GPS, reuses some tutorial code from a beginner Android book from around 2011.

  • SpeedFactor

    • The SpeedFactor app is a speedometer for yacht racing with audio feedback, so captain and crew do not have to look at their speedometer, and can hear immediate results to their sailing inputs.

    App Reuses some free tutorial code from Simplified Coding. Free download page published linkedin post 1 year ago LinkeIn Post. Published code reuse blog on Medium in How to make an App on November 10, 2020.

  • Bidhacker

    • The bidhacker platform for double-sided marketplaces uses smart algorithms to rate, match, facilitate, optimize, and automate transactions.
